# Mercury CS Micro-Learning: Banking Model & FDIC Insurance

A sample training module built as a portfolio piece for Mercury's Customer Support Learning & Enablement Specialist role.

## What it covers

Mercury is a fintech company, not an FDIC-insured bank. Banking services are provided through Choice Financial Group and Column N.A., Members FDIC. This is one of the most common questions Mercury's CS team fields — customers see the disclaimer, realize Mercury isn't a bank, and ask what happens to their money.

The module walks CS reps through:
- Mercury's banking model and how FDIC pass-through insurance works
- Three scenario-based conversations with drag-and-drop interactions
- A final knowledge check

## Design decisions

- Paginated, no scrolling — each step is a discrete interaction
- Drag-and-drop for scenarios with a click/tap fallback for mobile
- Flip cards to introduce the banking model concepts
- Built in plain HTML/CSS/JS, no frameworks
